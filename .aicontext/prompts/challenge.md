# Challenge

Review the current discussion, plan, or approach. Before we proceed, surface:

- **Questions** — anything unclear or underspecified
- **Assumptions** — things we're taking for granted that might be wrong
- **Concerns** — risks, edge cases, or things that could go wrong
- **Better ideas** — simpler approaches, alternatives we haven't considered

Be direct — disagreement is welcome, silence is not.
If everything genuinely looks good, say so briefly and move on.
